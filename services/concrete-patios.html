Concrete Patios

Create an outdoor oasis with a custom concrete patio perfect for entertaining.

Schedule this service

Overview

A concrete patio extends your living space and provides a durable area for relaxation and gatherings. We design patios that complement your landscape, from simple slabs to elaborate stamped designs. Our team ensures proper slope for drainage and a finish that resists staining.

What's included

  • Year-round outdoor enjoyment
  • Seamless integration with landscaping
  • Stain and weather resistant
  • Adds functional living space

How it works

1

Design Consultation

We discuss your patio goals, review design options, and help you choose the perfect shape, size, and finish.

2

Excavation & Base

We prepare the site by excavating and laying a compacted base to support the concrete and prevent shifting.

3

Installation & Sealing

We pour, finish, and seal the concrete to protect against the elements. We also add control joints to minimize cracking.

Common questions

How long does it take to install a concrete patio?

Most residential patios are completed in 2-4 days, depending on size and complexity. We'll provide a timeline during your consultation.

What maintenance does a concrete patio require?

Sealing every 2-3 years and regular cleaning with mild soap and water will keep your patio looking great. We offer resealing services.
