Concrete Foundations

Build on a solid foundation with our expert concrete foundation services.

Overview

A strong foundation is critical for any structure. We specialize in residential and commercial foundations, including slab-on-grade, stem wall, and pier and beam. Our team follows strict engineering standards to ensure stability and compliance with local codes.

What's included

  • Engineered for structural integrity
  • Compliant with Dallas building codes
  • Moisture protection and insulation options
  • Long-term peace of mind

How it works

1

Site Evaluation

We assess soil conditions, drainage, and load requirements to design the right foundation for your project.

2

Forming & Reinforcement

We set forms and install steel reinforcement (rebar) to provide strength and prevent cracking.

3

Pouring & Curing

Concrete is poured and carefully cured to achieve maximum strength. We monitor the process to ensure optimal results.

Common questions

Do you offer foundation repair?

Yes, we provide foundation repair services including underpinning, slab jacking, and crack injection to restore stability.

How long does a foundation need to cure before building?

Typically 7 days for light loads and 28 days for full strength. We'll advise based on your specific project.
